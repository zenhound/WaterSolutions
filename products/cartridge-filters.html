Cartridges

High-quality cartridge housings and replacement filters designed to maintain strong flow rates while delivering excellent filtration performance.

Big Blue Filter Housings

Big Blue housings are our preferred cartridge housings: tough, long-lasting, and large enough to maintain high flow rates. They feature attractive blue sumps with black tops and a red pressure-relief button for easy opening. Available in 10″ and 20″ sizes.

Big Blue manufacturer brochure (PDF)

NanoCeram-PAC

Developed by Argonide Corporation for NASA, NanoCeram cartridges deliver the flow rate of a 3-micron filter with filtration down to 0.2 microns. This allows removal or reduction of bacteria and many colloids (such as colloidal clay, a common local contaminant).

Installing a NanoCeram cartridge can improve ultraviolet and reverse osmosis system performance while reducing maintenance requirements. Available in Big Blue and standard cartridge sizes.
