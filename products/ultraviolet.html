Ultraviolet Systems

Ultraviolet (UV) disinfection systems provide reliable, chemical-free protection against bacteria, viruses, and microorganisms in your water supply.

Hallett Cactus UV System

The Hallett Cactus ultraviolet system uses patented Crossfire™ technology, positioning two UV lamps on opposite sides of the water stream. This design doubles UV intensity and significantly reduces the chance of bacterial breakthrough during periods of turbidity or prefilter failure.

The Cactus system supports flow rates up to 8 gallons per minute, making it suitable for most homes and small businesses. We install every system with a 5-micron prefilter, safety solenoid, and time-delay mechanism to ensure proper disinfection.

Hallett Upstream UV System

The Hallett Upstream is a fail-safe ultraviolet system designed to make bacterial breakthrough virtually impossible. In addition to Crossfire™ technology, the system includes continuous UV intensity monitoring, automated quartz sleeve cleaning, temperature regulation, and optional safety shutoff solenoids.

Capable of treating flow rates up to 10 gallons per minute, the Upstream is ideal for larger homes or demanding water conditions.

Hallett Upstream brochure (PDF)

Hallett 15 XS

The Hallett 15 XS delivers the same advanced monitoring and Crossfire™ technology as the Upstream but supports higher flow rates of up to 15 gallons per minute.

This system carries NSF approval, making it suitable for public water supplies such as restaurants, cafés, and other commercial applications.
