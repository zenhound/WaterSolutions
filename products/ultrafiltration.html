Ultrafiltration Systems

Ultrafiltration provides advanced, chemical-free filtration for your entire home, delivering clean, clear water at every tap.

PENTEK FreshPoint Ultrafiltration

The PENTEK FreshPoint Ultrafiltration System is a point-of-entry (POE) solution that filters all incoming water using hollow-fiber membrane technology. It is proven to reduce turbidity, particulates, and cysts while allowing beneficial minerals to remain in the water.

With a filtration rating down to 0.025 microns, the FreshPoint effectively removes contaminants smaller than bacteria, making it an excellent option for homes relying on well water.

The system is compact, low-maintenance, and can be configured to self-clean using filtered water, extending membrane life and reducing service requirements.
