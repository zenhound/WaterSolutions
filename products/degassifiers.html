Degassifiers

Degassifiers are designed to remove dissolved gases such as methane and hydrogen sulfide (sulfur) from well water without the use of chemicals.

Duke Aerator

The Duke Aerator is an effective solution for methane-bearing water and high levels of sulfur (hydrogen sulfide). Incoming water is dispersed through six vortex spray heads, creating a fine mist that maximizes gas release.

A high-volume air fan then strips sulfur and methane from the water, venting these gases safely outside through a dedicated vent pipe. The system includes a reliable Rusco spin-down prefilter to protect internal components.

After aeration, a 6 GPM submersible pump and bladder tank re-pressurize the water for delivery to your fixtures, ensuring consistent household water pressure.
