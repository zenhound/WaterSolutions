Water Softeners

Hard water causes scale buildup, soap inefficiency, appliance wear, and staining. Our water softening and conditioning systems are designed to solve these problems efficiently and reliably.

Charger Pro H2O 9100 Water Softener

The Charger Pro H2O 9100 is a traditional ion-exchange water softener that removes hardness minerals such as calcium and magnesium by exchanging them with sodium (or potassium) ions.

When the resin media becomes saturated, the system regenerates using a brine solution from the brine tank, restoring full softening capacity. This proven technology delivers consistently soft water throughout the home.

ScalePrep Conditioner (FilterSorb SP3)

The ScalePrep Conditioner uses FilterSorb SP3 media to treat hard water without salt, chemicals, or regeneration cycles.

This system transforms calcium and magnesium into harmless nano-sized crystals that pass through plumbing without adhering to pipes, fixtures, or heating elements. It is maintenance-free and does not waste water or require salt.
