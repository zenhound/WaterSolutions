Iron & Sulfur Filters

Our chemical-free iron and sulfur filtration systems eliminate odor, staining, and metallic taste while remaining low-maintenance and environmentally friendly.

Iron Breaker III

By far our most popular system, the Iron Breaker III is a powerful aeration-based filtration unit capable of treating high levels of iron and sulfur. It can also be configured to remove manganese, adjust pH, or address multiple water quality issues simultaneously.

Service intervals can range from once a year to as little as once per decade. Its chemical-free design adds nothing harmful to your drinking water or septic system and requires minimal floor space—often as little as a 10-inch diameter area.

Aer-Max System

The Aer-Max system is another high-performance aeration solution designed for iron and sulfur removal without chemical injection. It uses an aeration tank followed by filtration media to deliver excellent water quality.

This system supports higher flow rates and can be configured with twin alternating filter tanks to provide continuous 24-hour filtration—ideal for large homes or commercial applications.
