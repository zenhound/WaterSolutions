Filter Tanks

Flexible, serviceable filter tanks configured to match your specific water conditions.

FilterPrep Filter Tank Systems

FilterPrep filter tanks use the reliable Fleck 2510 SXT control valve and can be configured with a wide range of filtration media depending on your needs. These systems are commonly used for whole-house filtration and are designed for durability and consistent performance.

We most often use:

  • Activated carbon for chlorine removal and taste/odor improvement
  • Neutralizing marble for correcting low pH (acidic water)
  • ZeoPrep media for sediment reduction and softening applications

Filter tanks are typically serviced on a yearly basis. We’ll help determine the best media combination based on your water test results.
