Water Treatment Systems and Installation

Water Solutions of New Paltz designs and installs whole-house water treatment systems that make your water look clearer, smell better, and behave better throughout the home—showers, laundry, fixtures, and appliances. We specialize in chemical-free treatment options and bacterial protection for residential and commercial customers across the Hudson Valley.

Every property is different, so we start with a practical water test and a quick conversation about what you’re noticing: sulfur odor, rust staining, grit/sediment, scale buildup, dry skin, metallic taste, or concerns about well water safety. From there we build an “engineered chain” of treatment stages that solves the problem without overcomplicating the system.

Free consultation & water test

We’ll test your water, explain the results in plain language, and recommend a system sized to your home’s flow rate and your specific contaminants. No pressure—just clarity and a plan.

Common reasons customers call:

  • Odor: sulfur / “rotten egg” smell, especially hot water
  • Staining: iron rust stains in toilets, sinks, and laundry
  • Scale: mineral buildup on fixtures and in water heaters
  • Sediment: grit, cloudy water, clogged screens and cartridges
  • Well safety: peace-of-mind disinfection with UV when appropriate
